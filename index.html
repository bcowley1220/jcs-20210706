About Us
Third generation business offering a Gastro Pub experience to guests local and far. We serve dinner and brunch on Sunday's, a dance floor with live entertainment on the weekends, monthly wine tasting events as well as banquet rooms offering private gatherings from 12-160 people. Seasonally we offer patio dining from April - October. We are dedicated to providing the best possible experience for all of our guests, while honoring our Irish roots. Sláinte!
