Established in 1972, our third generation family business in Farmington, MI offers a Gastro Pub experience to every guest. We serve dinner and brunch on Sundays, host live entertainment on the weekends, and hold monthly wine tasting events as well as banquet rooms offering private gatherings from 12-160 people.


Seasonally we offer patio dining from April - October. We are dedicated to providing the best possible experience for all of our guests, while honoring our Irish roots. Sláinte!


Our banquet manager, Greg Cowley is available by email - greg@johncowleyandsons.com.There is also a contact form on the bottom of this page if you'd like to request more information.

Whether you're looking to host a small luncheon, party, memorial banquet or a wedding, we have done it all. We have audio & visual equipment available including a projector with a large screen. We offer a wide variety of banquet options; from customized menus to unique banquet rooms to suit your every need. We have a smaller side room downstairs (the Coolhenry Room) that can seat 12 people, our main downstairs room can seat 85, and our upstairs seats 125. Our staff is trained in banquet services to ensure that your function runs smoothly from the planning stages to the execution of your event.

There is 3 hour parking available in the parking lot behind our building as well as lots close by.
